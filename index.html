About TakeMySofa - what do we do?
It’s very simple, we remove unwanted armchairs and sofas. That’s it!
We are based in Ware (Hertfordshire) and we operate in Ware and Hertford and the surrounding nearby villages.
Call Matt on 07725 613854 or click the E-mail button in the corner to discuss your removal. See an indication of prices below.

What do we do with your old furnature?
This is also very simple, we will take it to a furniture recycling location eg: British Heart Foundation, or similar charity.
In some circumstances, depending on the condition of the sofa, we will need to dispose of it in a responsible manner.


Pricing

  We operate a very simple pricing structure and charge a single fixed fee per item depending on its size.  If you have more than one item to dispose of, on the same trip, for example if you want us to take a sofa and an armchair, then we are able to offer a 15% discount on the total cost.
